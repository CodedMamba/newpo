FAQ

Questions, answered

Is my data used to train the AI?
+
No. Your workspace data is never used to train any shared model. Nova AI runs on isolated, per-workspace context that is encrypted at rest and in transit.
Can I import my existing projects from Notion or Jira?
+
Yes — Nova has one-click importers for Notion, Jira, Linear, Asana, and Trello. Most imports complete in under five minutes regardless of project size.
How does the 14-day trial work?
+
You get full Pro access for 14 days with no credit card required. At the end of the trial you can choose a plan or downgrade to Starter — your data stays safe either way.
Do you offer discounts for non-profits or education?
+
Absolutely. Verified non-profits and accredited educational institutions receive 50% off all paid plans. Contact our team with proof of status to apply.
What happens if I exceed my plan limits?
+
We will notify you before you hit a limit and give you the option to upgrade. We never cut off access mid-sprint or charge surprise overages.
