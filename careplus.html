What We Offer

Comprehensive Medical Services

From preventive care to complex surgery, our multidisciplinary teams deliver excellence across every medical specialty.

General & Specialist Care

Board-certified physicians and subspecialists available for consultations, second opinions, and ongoing management.

Emergency & Trauma Unit

A fully equipped, round-the-clock emergency department with rapid triage and advanced life-support systems.

Cardiology & Cardiac Surgery

Minimally invasive procedures, ECGs, echocardiography, and full cardiac rehabilitation programmes.

Diagnostics & Imaging

MRI, CT, PET scans, ultrasound, and a fully digital pathology laboratory with same-day results.

Paediatrics & Neonatology

Dedicated child health services from newborn intensive care through to adolescent medicine.

Neurology & Neurosurgery

Advanced neurological diagnostics, stroke intervention, and complex brain and spine surgery.

Orthopaedics & Rehabilitation

Joint replacement, sports injury management, and structured physiotherapy programmes.

Pharmacy & Dispensary

In-house pharmacy with licensed pharmacists, 24-hour dispensing, and home-delivery options.

Our Story

Fifteen Years of Trusted Care

Founded in 2009, CarePlus Hospital was built on a single belief: that world-class healthcare should be accessible, dignified, and centred on the patient. From a single outpatient clinic, we have grown into a full-service tertiary hospital serving tens of thousands of patients annually.

Our medical team holds qualifications from leading institutions across Nigeria, the United Kingdom, and North America. We invest continuously in technology and training so that our patients receive the most current, evidence-based treatment available.
